Who We Are

A Premier Training Academy
for Life Science Professionals

AVB Life Science Academy is a premier industry-oriented training organization dedicated to life science students and professionals. We focus on bridging the gap between academic learning and real-time industry requirements.

Delivered large-scale virtual training to 200+ pharmacy students and professionals using immersive, tech-enabled sessions.

Conducted high-impact VR-based training programs for 200+ employees at reputed pharma companies in Hyderabad.

Organized multiple on-campus and off-campus job drives connecting trained candidates with various pharmaceutical companies.

Facilitated direct selections and offer letters through transparent, structured recruitment support for freshers and experienced candidates.

Provided intensive classroom trainings on GMP, QC, QA, documentation, and interview skills for life science graduates.

Designed customized training modules and internships for colleges, industries, and corporate clients in both online and offline modes.

Supported M.Pharm, B.Pharm, and M.Sc students with live projects, plagiarism checks, and publication assistance.

Offered government-linked and corporate skill-development programs with a focus on employability and industry compliance.

Shared regular pharma job updates, resume support, and profile recommendations to help candidates secure the right roles.

Positioned AVB Life Science Academy as a single platform for training, internships, corporate programs, and recruitment services.
